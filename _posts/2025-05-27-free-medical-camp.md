The free medical camp conducted at the PNP Wellness Center was inaugurated by Mrs. Mercy Devasya (President, Muttom Panchayat) and Sibigiri Church vicar Rev. Fr. John Palithottam. Sibigiri Church Asst. Vicar Rev. Fr. Johnson Pakkarampel and Mr. Mathew Joseph (Member, Muttom Panchayat) were also present. 

Services offered by the medical camp:

Physiotherapy: 
Back pain, knee pain, shoulder pain. 

Testing: 
Diabetes/blood sugar, BMI.

Advice on medications. 

Those who attended the camp have 20% discount on further treatment. 

Special health care for the monsoon season is available at PNP Wellness Center.
