Our clinic offers:

Diet and Nutrition

Guidance on healthy eating habits, including dietary restrictions and supplements.

Herbal Medicine

Herbs and other natural remedies to address specific health concerns.

Yoga and Exercise

Yoga and other forms of physical activity are used to improve flexibility, strength, and overall well-being.

Stress Management

Naturopathy addresses stress through techniques like meditation, relaxation exercises, and counseling.

Hydrotherapy

The use of water in various forms (hot, cold, steam) to promote healing.

Massage and Manipulative Therapies

These can include massage, acupressure, and other forms of physical therapy.

Accupuncture and Accupressure

Acupuncture and acupressure are both forms of Traditional Chinese Medicine that use pressure and needles to stimulate particular points in the body.
