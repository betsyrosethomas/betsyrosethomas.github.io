Our clinic offers:

Orthopaedic Physiotherapy

Focuses on musculoskeletal issues like injuries to bones, muscles, ligaments, and tendons.

Neurological Physiotherapy

Addresses disorders of the nervous system, such as stroke, multiple sclerosis, and Parkinson's disease.

Cardiorespiratory Physiotherapy

Deals with conditions affecting the heart and lungs, such as asthma and COPD.

Sports Physiotherapy

Addresses injuries and conditions related to athletic activities, including injury prevention, rehabilitation, and performance enhancement.

Paediatric Physiotherapy

Specializes in the assessment and treatment of children from infancy to adolescence.

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Focuses on managing age-related conditions such as osteoporosis, balance disorders, and mobility issues in older adults.

Pre-Operative Physiotherapy

Prepares patients for surgery by improving their strength, flexibility, and function.

Post-Surgical Physiotherapy

Assists with post-surgical rehabilitation by developing customized treatment plans that focus on restoring mobility, strength, and function after surgery.
